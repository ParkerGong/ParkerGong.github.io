Biography

Taiyuan Gong is a PhD candidate at the Advanced National Key Lab of Autonomous Operation for Railway Systems, Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU). His research focuses on edge intelligence, reinforcement learning, and generative AI/LLMs for railway perception–control–computing systems (ATO/VCTS & train–edge cooperative computing).
