About

Taiyuan Gong is currently a PhD candidate at the Advanced National Key Lab of Autonomous Operation for Railway Systems, Beijing Jiaotong University. His research focuses on edge intelligence, reinforcement learning, and generative AI for railway control and perception (ATO/VCTS, train–edge cooperative computing). From 2024–2025 He is a CSC-funded visiting PhD at SUTD, advised by Prof. Zehui Xiong.
