About

I am a PhD candidate at the Advanced National Key Lab of Autonomous Operation for Railway Systems, Beijing Jiaotong University. My research focuses on edge intelligence, reinforcement learning, and generative AI for railway control and perception (ATO/VCTS, train–edge cooperative computing). From 2024–2025 I am a CSC-funded visiting PhD at SUTD, advised by Prof. Zehui Xiong and Prof. Dusit Niyato.
